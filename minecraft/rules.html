Malainia Minecraft Server Rules

Rules for Gameplay, Game Chat, and Matrix Chat

  1. General

  • TurboHacker1024 reserves the right to change any rules at any time.

  • You can be banned or muted for violating any of these rules.

  • Treat others how you would want to be treated. Just be nice!

  • This list is not exhaustive. Other behaviors not on this list may still be deemed unacceptable.

  1. Gameplay

  • Do not kill players repeatedly without their permission, or in a manner that is upsetting.

  • Avoid griefing, stealing, killing, etc. TurboHacker1024 had no obligation to ban but reserves the right to ban you for upsetting other players based on these issues.

  • Do not grief terrain or intentionally create ugliness.

  • Repair creeper damage! DO IT IMMEDIATELY!

  • Claim your land! In a situation deemed reasonable by TurboHacker1024, he may take no action against griefing/stealing if you failed to claim your land.

  1. Chat

  • Keep the server PG.

  • Do not promote other Minecraft servers or other chat rooms.

  • Do not promote/post anything about Discord, Discord servers, or anything related to or associated with Discord.

  • No arguing or discussing politics/religion or any controversial topics unrelated to gameplay. Keep real life politics out of the game! PERIOD!

  • Do not bully, insult, or harass other players.

  • No cussing/cursing/swearing or bad jokes, not even implying it with alternative words/characters or abbreviations.

  • Do not fight in chat with other players. No one wants to hear your drama!
