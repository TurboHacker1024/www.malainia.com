Survival Server Rules

Rules for Server and Chat

  • Breaking any of these rules could result in a ban!
  • Treat others how you would want to be treated! Just be nice!
  • Do not fight in chat with other players. No one wants to hear your drama!
  • Do not kill other players without their 100% confirmed permission.
  • No arguing or discussing politics/religion or any controversial topics unrelated to gameplay.
  • Keep real life politics out of the game! PERIOD!
  • Do not bully, insult, or harass other players.
  • No cussing/cursing/swearing or bad jokes, not even implying it with alternative words/characters or abbreviations.
  • Keep it PG!
  • No griefing! This includes stealing, destroying, or killing animals and anything else mean.
  • Repair creeper damage! DO IT IMMEDIATELY!
  • If you get angry while playing then log off immediately. This server is to have fun only, and if it is not giving you that then do something better.
  • No wars are allowed on this server unless both sides peacefully agree to it first in real life.
  • TurboHacker1024, the server owner and admin, reserves the right to: Change any of these rules at any time, log all activity and messages on the server, ban any player for any reason or give no reason.
